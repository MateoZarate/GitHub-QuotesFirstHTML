WISH WEEK 2022


The Wish Week kid this year is a 9 year old boy named Walter.

Walter has Cuctic Fibrosis, which is an inherited disorder that causes severe damgae to the lings, digestive system and other organs in the body. Walter loves space and spends his time researching planets, stars, and solar systems. He likes to listen to classical music, play chess, and watch Star Wars and Odd Squad in his free time. His favorite color is sky blue and his favorite foods are Raisin Bran Cereal, chocolate ice cream, and french fries.

Attend Events with a wristband

Without a wristband, each event is $5. This includes all parents and family as well. Ages 4 and under get in free. The wristband does NOT get students into the basketball game on Friday. They may use their Student ID if they have the ACT card to receive ONE ticket prior to the game or pay $4 for a student ticket-$6 for adults. Every person (including adults) must have a ticket to enter the game, so please pay attention to when/where they will go on sale. Tickets for the game will be pre-sold during the week and will SELL OUT! You CANNOT show up to the Main Gym Saturday without a ticket. NOT ALL STUDENTS ARE GUARANTEED A TICKET!
